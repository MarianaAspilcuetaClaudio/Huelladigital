Digital Footprint

What is a Digital Footprint?

A digital footprint refers to the trail of data we create while using the internet. It includes the websites we visit, emails we send, social media interactions, online purchases, and more.It's the record of our online activities and interactions, which can be tracked and stored by various entities such as websites, internet service providers,advertisers. Digital footprints can have both positive and negative implications depending on how they are used and managed.

Tips to take care of your digital footprint

Some tips that you could use to take care of your digital footprint are delete all your gemail accounts that you dont use you could also limit your social media accounts and lastly you could set up stealth mode when you search stuff on a website or your personal device

Drawback of your digital footprint

Hackers today have access to massive amounts of personal information, available online and through social media. They can use this information to access consumers' bank information
